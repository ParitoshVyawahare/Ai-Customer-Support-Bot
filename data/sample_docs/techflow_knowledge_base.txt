TechFlow Solutions — Customer Support Knowledge Base
=====================================================

1. ABOUT TECHFLOW SOLUTIONS
---------------------------
TechFlow Solutions is a SaaS company founded in 2018, headquartered in Austin, Texas.
We provide workflow automation software for small and medium businesses. Our flagship
product is TechFlow Hub, an all-in-one platform for task management, team collaboration,
and process automation. We serve over 12,000 businesses across 40 countries.


2. PRICING PLANS
----------------
We offer three subscription plans:

Starter Plan: $19 per user per month (billed annually) or $24 per user per month (billed monthly)
  - Up to 10 users
  - 5 GB storage per user
  - Email support
  - Basic integrations (Google Workspace, Slack)

Professional Plan: $49 per user per month (billed annually) or $59 per user per month (billed monthly)
  - Up to 100 users
  - 50 GB storage per user
  - Priority email and chat support
  - Advanced integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot, Jira)
  - Custom workflows
  - API access

Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing (contact sales)
  - Unlimited users
  - Unlimited storage
  - Dedicated account manager
  - 24/7 phone support
  - SSO/SAML authentication
  - SOC 2 Type II compliance
  - 99.99% uptime SLA

All plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.


3. REFUND POLICY
----------------
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all annual subscriptions. If you are not
satisfied with TechFlow Hub within the first 30 days of your annual subscription,
contact billing@techflow.example and we will issue a full refund within 5-7 business days.

Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable but can be canceled at any time. Cancellations
take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

Enterprise contracts have custom refund terms specified in the master services agreement.

5. DATA SECURITY AND COMPLIANCE
-------------------------------
TechFlow Solutions is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. All customer data is
encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3. We perform daily backups
that are retained for 30 days. Our infrastructure runs on AWS in the US-East and
EU-West regions, with full data residency options available on the Enterprise plan.

We do NOT sell customer data to third parties. We do NOT train AI models on customer data.


6. INTEGRATIONS
---------------
TechFlow Hub integrates with: Google Workspace, Slack, Microsoft Teams, Zoom, Salesforce,
HubSpot, Jira, GitHub, GitLab, Asana, Trello, Notion, Zapier, Make.com, Stripe, QuickBooks,

Salesforce, HubSpot, and Jira integrations require the Professional plan or higher.


7. ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT
---------------------
To change your plan: Log in, go to Settings > Billing > Change Plan. Upgrades take effect
immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

To add users: Settings > Team > Invite Members. You will be billed pro-rated for new users
added mid-cycle.

To delete your account: Settings > Account > Delete Account. This permanently removes all
data after a 30-day grace period during which you can restore the account by emailing
support@techflow.example.

Password reset: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. A reset link is sent
to your registered email address and is valid for 1 hour.


8. UPTIME AND OUTAGES
---------------------
We guarantee 99.9% uptime on Professional plans and 99.99% on Enterprise. Status updates
are available at status.techflow.example. In the event of a service outage exceeding 1 hour,
affected customers receive service credits according to our SLA.


9. MOBILE APPS
--------------

and biometric login.
